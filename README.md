# Sickle Cell Home-Based Pain Management Study

## Project Timeline
Summer 2025

## Project Overview
This IRB-approved study examined home-based pain management strategies among individuals with sickle cell disease. The objective was to understand self-management behaviors and identify opportunities for patient-centered interventions.

## Role
Principal Student Investigator

## Methods
- Developed study protocol
- Designed structured survey instrument
- Coordinated participant recruitment
- Managed data collection
- Conducted quantitative statistical analysis

## Tools Used
- Survey design methodology
- Statistical analysis software
- IRB human subjects compliance procedures

## Key Contributions
- Independent research leadership
- Data management and analysis
- Ethical research compliance
- Results synthesis and reporting

Study approved by Institutional Review Board. All data were de-identified.
